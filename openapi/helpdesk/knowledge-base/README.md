## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Customers ask the same questions repeatedly, support teams answer the same questions over and over, and knowledge is trapped in support agents' heads. Support is expensive, customers wait, and satisfaction suffers. You're answering questions reactively instead of enabling customers proactively.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Repeated Questions**: Same questions asked repeatedly, support teams waste time
- **Knowledge Trapped**: Knowledge exists only in support agents' heads
- **No Self-Service**: Customers can't find answers, must contact support
- **Outdated Documentation**: Documentation is outdated, customers get wrong answers
- **No Search**: Can't search documentation effectively, information is hard to find
- **High Support Costs**: Support teams overwhelmed with basic questions
