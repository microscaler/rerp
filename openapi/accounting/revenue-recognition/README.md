# Revenue Recognition

## What It Is

The Revenue Recognition service is your deferral and recognition engine - the system that turns invoices, contracts, schedules, and accounting rules into controlled revenue and expense recognition. It ensures income and costs are recognized in the right period, with the right evidence, instead of being posted only when an invoice is issued.

## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Subscription revenue, prepaid services, annual contracts, implementation fees, deferred costs, and multi-period arrangements are difficult to account for correctly. Teams use spreadsheets to spread revenue over time, then manually post journals and hope the schedule matches the contract.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Spreadsheet Deferrals**: Recognition schedules are calculated outside the accounting system
- **Period Errors**: Revenue or expenses land in the wrong accounting period
- **No Rule Consistency**: Similar contracts are recognized differently by different users
- **Manual Journal Entries**: Recognition postings are created by hand
- **Weak Audit Evidence**: It is hard to explain why revenue was recognized in a period
- **Change Complexity**: Contract modifications and cancellations break existing schedules

## How It Delivers Pain Relief

### 📐 **Recognition Rules**
Define reusable rules for straight-line, milestone, usage, contract, deferred revenue, and deferred expense recognition patterns.

### 📅 **Recognition Schedules**
Generate schedules from invoices, contracts, and rules. Finance can see what will be recognized, when, and why.

### 📦 **Deferred Revenue And Expense**
Track deferred balances as first-class accounting records rather than hidden spreadsheet calculations.

### ▶️ **Recognition Runs**
Execute periodic recognition runs that create controlled posting outputs and preserve execution history.

### 🔍 **Audit-Ready Traceability**
Trace recognized amounts back to source invoices, rules, schedules, runs, and generated journal entries.

### 🎯 **Business Impact**
- **Improve Revenue Accuracy**: Recognize revenue in the right period
- **Reduce Manual Journals**: Automate recurring recognition postings
- **Support Growth Models**: Handle subscriptions, prepaid contracts, and recurring services
- **Strengthen Audit Evidence**: Explain every recognized amount with source and schedule

## Key Capabilities

- **Recognition Rules**: Reusable recognition logic for revenue and expense patterns
- **Recognition Schedules**: Schedule generation, lines, balances, and source references
- **Deferred Revenue**: Deferred income tracking, release schedules, and accounting references
- **Deferred Expenses**: Prepaid and deferred cost recognition with schedule control
- **Recognition Runs**: Periodic execution, status, outputs, and posting handoff
- **Accounting Integration**: Links to invoices, GL postings, reporting, and future contract/subscription modules

## Perfect For

- Subscription and recurring revenue businesses
- Companies with annual contracts, prepaid services, or implementation fees
- Finance teams needing ASC 606 or IFRS 15-style discipline
- Organizations currently managing deferrals in spreadsheets
- Enterprises requiring recognition evidence for audit
