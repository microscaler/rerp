# Documents Extraction

## What It Is

The Documents Extraction service is your accounting document automation hub - the system that ingests source documents, classifies them, extracts structured accounting data, routes uncertain results for human review, and links approved documents to invoices, bills, bank statements, and EDI records.

## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Accounting teams still receive invoices, bills, receipts, bank documents, and statutory files as PDFs, scans, emails, and uploads. People re-key the same data into accounting screens, attach files manually, and lose the relationship between the source document and the accounting record.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Manual Data Entry**: Staff retype supplier, customer, tax, amount, and line information from documents
- **Slow Invoice Processing**: Documents wait in inboxes before they become accounting records
- **Poor Traceability**: Attachments exist, but extracted values and review decisions are not auditable
- **Inconsistent Classification**: Bills, invoices, statements, and EDI files are routed manually
- **Weak Review Controls**: Low-confidence extraction results are not handled with clear approval states
- **Disconnected Documents**: Source files are not reliably linked to invoices, bills, statements, or submissions

## How It Delivers Pain Relief

### 📥 **Document Intake**
Capture accounting documents from uploads, inboxes, EDI, bank feeds, and future connectors. Every document gets an identity and lifecycle.

### 🧠 **Classification And Extraction**
Classify document type and extract structured accounting fields: parties, dates, references, amounts, taxes, lines, currencies, and confidence scores.

### ✅ **Human Review Loop**
Route low-confidence or high-risk extraction results for review and approval. Automation stays controlled instead of blindly posting bad data.

### 🔗 **Accounting Linkage**
Link approved documents to customer invoices, vendor bills, bank statements, EDI submissions, or audit packs. The source stays attached to the accounting outcome.

### 🔍 **Audit-Ready Evidence**
Preserve original files, extracted fields, confidence, reviewer decisions, and linkage history. Every automated value can be explained.

### 🎯 **Business Impact**
- **Reduce Manual Entry**: Automate capture of common accounting fields
- **Speed Up Processing**: Move documents from inbox to accounting workflow faster
- **Improve Control**: Review uncertain extraction before it affects the books
- **Strengthen Audit Trails**: Keep source, extraction, review, and accounting linkage together

## Key Capabilities

- **Accounting Documents**: Upload, classify, store, search, and track source documents
- **Extraction Jobs**: OCR, parsing, structured extraction, confidence scoring, and job status
- **Review Workflow**: Human validation, approval, rejection, correction, and review history
- **Document Linkage**: Connect documents to invoices, vendor bills, bank statements, EDI documents, and audit packs
- **Confidence Models**: Capture field-level confidence and route exceptions for review
- **Automation Foundation**: Supports invoice extraction, bill extraction, bank statement support, and statutory document evidence

- Organizations needing traceable document-to-accounting workflows
- Teams adopting EDI, OCR, or AI-assisted accounting automation
- Enterprises requiring source-document evidence for audits
