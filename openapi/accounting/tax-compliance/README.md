# Tax Compliance

## What It Is

The Tax Compliance service is your statutory tax control center - the system that manages tax periods, jurisdiction rules, tax returns, payments, filings, and audit packs. It turns tax from scattered spreadsheets and calendar reminders into a governed accounting workflow with evidence, status, and submission traceability.

## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Tax compliance is fragmented across spreadsheets, accounting exports, email trails, and local filing portals. Every jurisdiction has different rules, deadlines, formats, and evidence requirements. Missed filings, late payments, and unsupported numbers create audit risk and penalties.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Manual Tax Calendars**: Filing periods and due dates are tracked outside the accounting system
- **Jurisdiction Complexity**: VAT, GST, sales tax, withholding, and local regimes all behave differently
- **Weak Evidence Packs**: Supporting transactions and calculations are hard to reproduce during audits
- **Late Or Incorrect Filings**: Tax returns are assembled manually and reviewed too late
- **No Filing Lifecycle**: Draft, validated, submitted, accepted, rejected, paid, and amended states are not controlled
- **Hardcoded Local Rules**: Country behavior becomes embedded in core accounting instead of reusable packs

## How It Delivers Pain Relief

### 🗓️ **Controlled Tax Periods**
Define tax periods, filing windows, deadlines, and jurisdictions in one place. Finance teams know what is due, when it is due, and what status each return is in.

### ⚖️ **Jurisdiction Rule Management**
Model tax rules, return profiles, rates, thresholds, and effective dates without bloating the general ledger. Country packs can extend the service safely.

### 📄 **Return Lifecycle**
Create, validate, submit, amend, and track statutory tax returns with clear statuses. Every return becomes a durable accounting artifact.

### 💳 **Tax Payment Tracking**
Connect filed returns to tax payments, settlement references, and bank reconciliation. Compliance does not stop at submission.

### 🔍 **Audit Evidence Packs**
Generate audit packs with source transactions, calculations, adjustments, submissions, and payment evidence. Auditors get a reproducible trail.

### 🎯 **Business Impact**
- **Reduce Penalty Risk**: Controlled periods and due dates reduce missed filings
- **Improve Audit Readiness**: Evidence packs make return numbers defensible
- **Scale Localization**: Country packs extend tax behavior without rewriting core accounting
- **Shorten Filing Cycles**: Returns move through visible review and submission states

## Key Capabilities

- **Tax Periods**: Filing calendars, reporting periods, due dates, and company/jurisdiction scope
- **Tax Rules**: Jurisdiction-specific rules, tax types, effective dates, and return profiles
- **Tax Returns**: Draft, validation, submission, acceptance, rejection, amendment, and closure lifecycle
- **Tax Payments**: Payment tracking, settlement status, due amounts, and reconciliation references
- **Audit Packs**: Evidence packages for statutory filings, source balances, calculations, and supporting documents
- **Localization Extension Point**: Foundation for VAT, GST, sales tax, SAF-T, HMRC, 1099, OSS, and other country packs

- Finance teams that need controlled statutory return workflows
- Organizations preparing for audits or regulatory inspections
- ERP implementations that need country packs without hardcoding localization into core accounting
- Enterprises requiring traceable tax filings and payment evidence
