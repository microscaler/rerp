## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Your accounting is fragmented. Transactions are recorded in different systems, journal entries are manual, and you can't trust your financial reports. - **Manual Journal Entries**: Recording transactions manually is slow, error-prone, and time-consuming
- **Unbalanced Books**: Debits and credits don't match, finding errors takes hours
- **Slow Month-End Close**: Closing books takes weeks instead of days
- **No Audit Trail**: Can't trace transactions or prove accuracy to auditors
- **Multi-Company Complexity**: Managing multiple companies with inter-company transactions is chaotic
- **Currency Confusion**: Multi-currency transactions are calculated incorrectly
