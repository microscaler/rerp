# Treasury

## What It Is

The Treasury service is your cash planning and liquidity command center - the system that turns bank balances, receivables, payables, forecasts, bank relationships, and planned transfers into a clear view of where cash is, where it is going, and what action finance should take.

## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Cash decisions are made from stale bank balances, disconnected spreadsheets, and optimistic forecasts. Finance teams know what posted yesterday, but not whether next week's payroll, supplier run, tax payment, and collections timing will create a cash gap.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Fragmented Cash View**: Bank balances, AR, AP, and forecasts live in different systems
- **Reactive Liquidity Management**: Cash shortfalls are discovered too late
- **Manual Forecasting**: Forecasts are rebuilt by hand and rarely match operational data
- **Poor Bank Relationship Tracking**: Facilities, accounts, limits, and contacts are not managed centrally
- **Uncontrolled Transfers**: Cash movements lack planning, approval, and audit context
- **No Scenario Planning**: Finance cannot compare conservative, expected, and optimistic liquidity outcomes

## How It Delivers Pain Relief

### 💰 **Cash Positioning**
Combine bank balances, pending transfers, receivables, payables, and expected flows into a consolidated cash position.

### 📈 **Cash Forecasts**
Create short-, medium-, and long-range forecasts using operational accounting data instead of spreadsheet guesswork.

### 🧭 **Liquidity Plans**
Turn forecasts into funding recommendations, transfer plans, and liquidity actions. Manage cash proactively.

### 🏦 **Bank Relationship Management**
Track bank accounts, facilities, limits, contacts, and relationship metadata so treasury context is visible.

### 🔁 **Controlled Cash Transfers**
Plan, approve, execute, and audit cash transfers between accounts, companies, and banking partners.

### 🎯 **Business Impact**
- **Improve Cash Visibility**: See current and projected cash in one place
- **Avoid Surprises**: Detect liquidity gaps before payment deadlines arrive
- **Make Better Funding Decisions**: Use forecasts and scenarios to guide action
- **Control Transfers**: Keep cash movements planned, approved, and traceable

## Key Capabilities

- **Cash Positions**: Consolidated cash snapshots by company, account, bank, currency, and date
- **Cash Forecasts**: Forecast assumptions, horizons, expected inflows/outflows, and scenario support
- **Liquidity Plans**: Funding recommendations, planned actions, approvals, and liquidity status
- **Bank Relationships**: Bank metadata, accounts, facilities, contacts, limits, and relationship context
- **Cash Transfers**: Planned and executed transfers with approval and audit trail
- **Accounting Integration**: Forecast inputs from bank sync, AR, AP, tax payments, and payment batches

## Perfect For

- Businesses managing multiple bank accounts, entities, or currencies
- Finance teams needing forward-looking cash visibility
- Organizations with frequent supplier runs, payroll, tax, or funding events
- Companies moving from bookkeeping to active liquidity management
- Enterprises needing controlled cash transfer planning
