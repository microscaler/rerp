## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Paying vendors is chaotic. Invoices arrive through email, mail, and portals, but there's no central system to manage them. Payments are processed manually, approvals are ad-hoc, and you can't track what you owe. Late payments damage vendor relationships, early payments tie up cash, and duplicate payments waste money.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Invoice Chaos**: Vendor invoices scattered across email, mail, and systems
- **Manual Payment Processing**: Processing payments manually is slow and error-prone
- **No Approval Process**: Payments happen without proper authorization
- **Duplicate Payments**: Same invoices paid twice, money wasted
- **Late Payment Penalties**: Missing payment terms leads to late fees and damaged relationships
- **No AP Visibility**: Can't see what you owe, when it's due, or who approved it
