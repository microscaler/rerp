# Lease Accounting

## What It Is

The Lease Accounting service is your lease compliance and lifecycle engine - the system that manages lease contracts, payment schedules, lease liabilities, right-of-use assets, modifications, and remeasurements. It turns lease accounting from spreadsheet math into controlled, auditable accounting workflows.

## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Lease accounting is complex and easy to get wrong. Teams track leases in spreadsheets, calculate liabilities manually, create right-of-use assets by hand, and struggle when leases are modified, renewed, terminated, or remeasured.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Spreadsheet Lease Registers**: Lease details and schedules are stored outside the ERP
- **Manual Liability Calculations**: Present value, interest, and amortization are calculated by hand
- **Disconnected Assets**: Right-of-use assets are not linked cleanly to lease liabilities
- **Modification Complexity**: Renewals, terminations, and changes require recalculation and manual journals
- **Compliance Risk**: IFRS 16 and ASC 842-style reporting requires traceable schedules and disclosures
- **Weak Audit Trail**: Auditors cannot easily trace lease balances to contracts and assumptions

## How It Delivers Pain Relief

### 📑 **Lease Contract Management**
Capture lease terms, start/end dates, payment patterns, currencies, rates, classifications, and accounting assumptions.

### 📆 **Payment Schedules**
Generate lease payment schedules that drive liability, interest, and asset accounting.

### ⚖️ **Lease Liabilities**
Track liability balances, amortization, interest, payments, and remeasurement outputs.

### 🏗️ **Right-Of-Use Assets**
Connect lease contracts to right-of-use assets, depreciation, impairments, and lifecycle changes.

### 🔁 **Modification Workflows**
Handle lease modifications, extensions, terminations, remeasurements, and accounting impacts with reviewable workflows.

### 🎯 **Business Impact**
- **Reduce Compliance Risk**: Make lease schedules and assumptions auditable
- **Improve Accuracy**: Automate liability and right-of-use asset calculations
- **Control Changes**: Treat modifications as governed accounting events
- **Shorten Close Work**: Generate repeatable lease accounting outputs each period

## Key Capabilities

- **Leases**: Contract setup, classification, terms, assumptions, and lifecycle status
- **Lease Payment Schedules**: Payment lines, dates, amounts, currencies, and schedule status
- **Lease Liabilities**: Liability balances, interest, amortization, payments, and remeasurement tracking
- **Right-Of-Use Assets**: Asset creation, depreciation links, carrying values, and lifecycle state
- **Lease Modifications**: Extensions, terminations, rate changes, remeasurements, and accounting outputs
- **Accounting Integration**: Links to asset accounting, AP payments, GL postings, and disclosure reporting

- Finance teams needing IFRS 16 or ASC 842-style lease controls
- Organizations currently managing leases in spreadsheets
- Companies with frequent lease modifications or renewals
- Enterprises requiring auditable lease liability and asset schedules
