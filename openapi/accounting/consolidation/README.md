# Consolidation

## What It Is

The Consolidation service is your group accounting engine - the system that brings multiple companies together into controlled consolidation groups, runs consolidation cycles, generates elimination entries, and produces group reporting packs that explain how entity-level books became group-level financial statements.

## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Multi-company reporting is slow, manual, and fragile. Finance teams export ledgers from each entity, translate currencies in spreadsheets, eliminate intercompany balances by hand, and struggle to explain changes between local books and consolidated results.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Manual Group Reporting**: Consolidated statements are assembled outside the ERP
- **Intercompany Noise**: Related-party balances and transactions inflate group results
- **Currency Translation Complexity**: Entity currencies and group currencies are handled manually
- **Weak Ownership Modeling**: Group structures, subsidiaries, and consolidation scope are unclear
- **No Consolidation Audit Trail**: Adjustments and eliminations are hard to trace
- **Slow Close**: Group reporting waits until every spreadsheet is reconciled

## How It Delivers Pain Relief

### 🏢 **Consolidation Groups**
Define which companies belong in a group, how they are included, and which reporting periods are consolidated.

### ▶️ **Consolidation Runs**
Execute repeatable consolidation cycles with status, scope, inputs, outputs, and audit history.

### 🔄 **Elimination Rules**
Configure rules for intercompany receivables, payables, revenue, expense, investment, and equity eliminations.

### 🧾 **Generated Elimination Entries**
Create traceable elimination entries that explain how entity results become group results.

### 📦 **Group Reporting Packs**
Produce durable reporting artifacts for finance leadership, auditors, and statutory group reporting.

### 🎯 **Business Impact**
- **Faster Group Close**: Reduce spreadsheet consolidation work
- **Better Control**: Make eliminations repeatable and reviewable
- **Improve Auditability**: Trace group numbers back to entities, rules, and entries
- **Scale Multi-Company Reporting**: Support growth beyond single-entity accounting

## Key Capabilities

- **Consolidation Groups**: Company group definitions, scope, reporting currency, and consolidation setup
- **Consolidation Runs**: Periodic execution, status tracking, input selection, and result history
- **Elimination Rules**: Rules for intercompany balances, transactions, equity, and investment eliminations
- **Elimination Entries**: Generated accounting entries with source references and review status
- **Group Reporting Packs**: Consolidated statements, supporting schedules, and evidence packages
- **Intercompany Foundation**: Supports future matching, dispute resolution, settlement, and close integration

## Perfect For

- Multi-company groups needing consolidated financial statements
- Businesses with intercompany transactions and balances
- Finance teams moving away from spreadsheet consolidation
- Organizations preparing for audit or investor reporting
- Enterprises requiring controlled group close and reporting evidence
