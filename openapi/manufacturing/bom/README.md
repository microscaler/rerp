# Bill of Materials (BOM)

## What It Is

The Bill of Materials service is your business's product structure engine‚Äîthe system that manages product recipes, component relationships, and manufacturing configurations. It's the BOM platform that transforms product complexity into structured, manageable manufacturing instructions.

## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Product structures are complex and chaotic. You can't see what goes into products, how components relate, or what manufacturing steps are required. BOMs are in spreadsheets, versions are lost, and costs are unknown. You're building products blindly instead of building them systematically.

**The Pain Points**:
- **BOM Chaos**: Product structures in spreadsheets, always outdated
- **No Version Control**: Can't track BOM versions or changes
- **Cost Unknown**: Don't know component costs or total product costs
- **Complex Configurations**: Multi-level products with sub-assemblies are unmanageable
- **No Routing Information**: Manufacturing steps and work centers not defined
- **Component Confusion**: Can't see alternative components or substitutes
