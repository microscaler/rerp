## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Managing documents is chaotic and inefficient. Documents are stored in filing cabinets, email attachments, and local drives. Finding documents is difficult, versions are lost, and access is uncontrolled. Documents are disorganized instead of organized.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Document Chaos**: Documents scattered across filing cabinets, email, and drives
- **Version Confusion**: Can't track document versions or changes
- **Access Control Missing**: Can't control who accesses documents
- **No Search**: Can't search documents effectively, information is hard to find
- **Document Loss**: Documents get lost, versions are overwritten
- **No Collaboration**: Teams can't collaborate on documents effectively
