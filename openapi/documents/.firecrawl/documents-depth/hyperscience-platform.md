## IntelligentAutomation  fortheBackOffice

Hyperscience Hypercell is a fully integrated AI platform that transforms back-office operations and enterprise decision-making. With its modular, composable AI architecture, Hypercell features powerful enterprise capabilities such as FedRAMP High certification for key public sector workloads, multi-vendor cloud support with AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform, as well native generative AI code blocks to teach gen AI the language of your business.

### Secure,scalable,andenterprise-ready

Hypercell is designed to evolve with your business, adapting to new document types, regulatory changes, and workflow complexities without costly retraining.

Our proprietary model-based architecture enables seamless integration with existing enterprise systems and LLMs, unlocking AI-informed automation without disrupting operations. With governance, auditability, and security at its core, Hypercell ensures enterprises can scale automation with confidence—no matter the volume or variety of documents they process.

## Build,customize,andscalewithblocksandflows

![Branches](https://cdn-ileemmj.nitrocdn.com/wALvQbbEdpJYTPxhimfIBLPgPVyNqPtS/assets/images/optimized/rev-35c1bd1/www.hyperscience.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/blocks-flows.svg)

#### Blocks and flows

Build, modify, and scale workflows effortlessly with a low-code interface, ensuring automation adapts to evolving processes while maintaining full control and transparency. Blocks represent discrete processing functions in the Hypercell - such as document ingestion, classification, extraction, validation, business rule enforcement, decisioning, and LLM-powered analysis - that can be connected and arranged into Flows to meet unique business requirements.

![API First](https://cdn-ileemmj.nitrocdn.com/wALvQbbEdpJYTPxhimfIBLPgPVyNqPtS/assets/images/optimized/rev-35c1bd1/www.hyperscience.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/api-first.svg)

#### API-first approach

Ensure seamless integration with downstream systems, enterprise applications, and business intelligence tools with the API-first architecture of Hypercell. By treating integrations as configurable Blocks within the workflow engine, organizations can easily connect Hypercell to RPA platforms, databases, content management systems, and custom applications—without complex development work. These integration Blocks enable data to flow effortlessly between systems, ensuring extracted, validated, and enriched information reaches the right destinations in real time. With this flexible, modular approach, enterprises can modernize document-driven workflows while maintaining interoperability across their technology stack.

![Custom Code](https://cdn-ileemmj.nitrocdn.com/wALvQbbEdpJYTPxhimfIBLPgPVyNqPtS/assets/images/optimized/rev-35c1bd1/www.hyperscience.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/custom-code.svg)

#### Custom code

Embed business logic, apply advanced transformations, and integrate proprietary models directly within workflows with Custom Code Blocks. With this flexible approach, organizations can build custom validation rules, implement decisioning logic, enrich extracted data, and trigger automated actions—all within the Hypercell platform. By seamlessly incorporating bespoke logic into Flows, businesses can tailor automation to their unique requirements, ensuring greater control, adaptability, and extensibility across document-driven processes.
