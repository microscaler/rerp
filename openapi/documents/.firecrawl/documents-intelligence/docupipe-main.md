DocuPipe is an AI-powered document processing solution that specializes in accurate data extraction from diverse document types, such as invoices, medical records, insurance claims, and shipping documents. The platform allows users to define custom extraction rules to optimize data retrieval from complex documents. With security certifications including SOC 2 and ISO 27001, DocuPipe assures users of data privacy and compliance with regulations like GDPR and HIPAA. The service boasts capabilities for handling handwriting, tables, and various formats, with features designed for speed and efficiency. It is widely used across industries, processing over one billion pages to date.
