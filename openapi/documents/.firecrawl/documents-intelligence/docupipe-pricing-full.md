#### How credits work

Credits are the currency that powers DocuPipe. Different services cost different amounts, but the most common workflow is simple.

###### Most common workflow: Parse + Standardize

Most users upload documents (Parse) and then extract structured data from them (Standardize). Both are charged per page, so together they cost **3 credits per page** — 1 to parse, 2 to standardize.

#### Frequently asked questions

###### What happens if I run out of credits?

On the Business and Premium plans, you can continue processing documents at the overage rate. On the Starter plan, you'll need to wait for your credits to reset next month or upgrade.

###### Can I cancel anytime?

On the monthly plan, yes — you can cancel at any time and there will be no further charges.

###### What file formats are supported?

DocuPipe supports PDF, images (PNG, JPG, TIFF), CSV, Excel, Word, HTML, plain text, JSON, and XML.

###### Do credits roll over to the next month?

No, unused credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. This keeps pricing simple and predictable.

###### Is there a free trial?

The Starter plan is free forever with 100 credits/month plus a 300-credit sign-up bonus. No credit card required.

###### What compliance certifications do you have?

DocuPipe is SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified and offers BAA agreements for HIPAA compliance on all paid plans.
