DocuPipe is an AI-powered document intelligence solution designed to accurately extract and structure data from various document types. It supports diverse industries such as finance, healthcare, logistics, and real estate, enabling businesses to overcome challenges like handwriting, complex formatting, and language barriers. Key features include custom extraction configurations, stringent security protocols (SOC-2 type 2, ISO 27001 certifications), and compliance with GDPR and HIPAA. The platform offers flexibility and speed in processing and has handled over 1 billion pages. Clients enjoy a user-friendly experience with high ratings on review platforms.
