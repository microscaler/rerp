DocuPipe offers AI-powered document intelligence solutions with transparent pricing. It provides a free Starter plan with 100 credits/month and additional paid plans tailored for growing teams, scalable operations, and large enterprises. Key services include document parsing and data standardization, charged on a credit basis. Users can process various document types (PDF, images, etc.) and are guaranteed compliance with SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 standards. Additional features include XML and JSON support, an auto-generated schema service, and an FAQ section addressing credits usage and compliance certifications.
