Nanonets provides AI-driven automation tools aimed at enhancing efficiency in complex business processes such as order processing, payment handling, and contract analysis. Their agents integrate seamlessly across existing systems (ERPs, emails, and approval chains) to automate tasks, significantly reducing manual workload. Features include multi-format invoice capture, automated approval workflows, and robust security controls. Nanonets' solutions are trusted by major companies like Volkswagen and Roche, offering substantial cost reductions and operational improvements. The platform emphasizes ease of deployment without the need for major system changes and provides enterprise-grade security focusing on compliance with standards such as SOC 2 and GDPR.
