Docparser offers a range of subscription plans for document parsing services aimed at automating data extraction from various document types such as PDFs, Word files, and more. Key plans include: 
- **Starter Plan**: $39/month (100 parsing credits, up to 15 parsers, basic features). 
- **Professional Plan**: $61.50/month (3000 credits, up to 50 parsers, additional features). 
- **Business Plan**: $133/month (12000 credits, up to 500 parsers, priority support). 
- **Enterprise Plan**: Custom pricing for tailored solutions. 
Each plan provides options for integrations, support resources, and add-ons like Multifactor Authentication and Extended Document Retention.
