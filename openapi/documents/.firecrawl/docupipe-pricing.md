DocuPipe offers AI-powered document intelligence services with a range of pricing plans designed to cater to different user needs. The pricing includes a free Starter plan, a Business plan at $99/month, a Premium plan at $499/month, and a customizable Enterprise plan. Users utilize credits to access features such as document parsing and data standardization, with costs varying per page based on services used. Various additional features are available at specific credit charges, including automated schema generation, analysis, and review. Compliance certifications include SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and HIPAA for paid plans. The platform supports multiple file formats and allows users to manage credits effectively, with details on credit overages and cancellation policies.
