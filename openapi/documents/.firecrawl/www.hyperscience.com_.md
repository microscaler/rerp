Hyperscience is recognized as a leader in Intelligent Document Processing (IDP) by multiple major industry analysts, including Gartner, Forrester, and IDC. The company provides a platform known as Hypercell that automates workflows, reduces errors, and accelerates process outcomes. Their recent Spring 2026 release emphasizes a transition from IDP to Intelligent Inference, enhancing AI capabilities in enterprise workflows. Key products include solutions for Freight Pay and SNAP, catering to various industries such as finance, healthcare, and public sector. Hyperscience focuses on achieving high accuracy rates (99.5%), ensuring security and compliance, and integrating with existing technology. They also promote the idea of training generative AI models with business-specific data for enhanced accuracy and relevance.
