DocuPipe offers an AI-powered document intelligence solution that accurately extracts data from various document types, such as invoices, medical records, insurance claims, and contracts. It enables users to customize extraction rules tailored to their business needs, supporting complex document layouts, handwriting, and multiple languages. The platform ensures security and compliance with certifications like SOC-2 type 2 and ISO 27001. DocuPipe is designed for various industries, including finance, healthcare, and logistics, and has processed over one billion pages. The service emphasizes speed, accuracy, and user-defined extraction workflows.
