## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Your product information is scattered across spreadsheets, websites, catalogs, and systems. Sales teams use outdated pricing. Marketing promotes discontinued products. E-commerce shows wrong inventory. Every department has a different version of the truth, and customers pay the price.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Data Chaos**: Product information in 10 different places, never matching
- **Outdated Information**: Sales teams quoting prices from last year's spreadsheet
- **Inventory Disconnects**: Website shows "in stock" but warehouse is empty
- **Manual Updates**: Changing a product description means updating 5 different systems
- **Lost Sales**: Customers can't find products because they're not properly categorized
- **Compliance Risk**: Selling expired products or products with incorrect specifications
