## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Your business is drowning in password chaos. Employees juggle multiple logins, forget passwords constantly, and security breaches happen because authentication is fragmented across systems. You're spending IT resources on password resets instead of innovation.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Password Fatigue**: Employees manage 10+ different passwords, leading to security shortcuts
- **Security Vulnerabilities**: Weak passwords, shared credentials, and no centralized security policy
- **IT Overhead**: Help desk overwhelmed with password reset requests
- **Compliance Risk**: No unified audit trail of who accessed what and when
- **User Frustration**: Locked accounts, forgotten passwords, and slow access to critical systems
