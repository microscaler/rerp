# Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

## What It Is

The Role-Based Access Control service is your business's digital security guard, ensuring the right people have access to the right information at the right time. It's the intelligent permission system that protects your sensitive data while enabling productivity.

## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Your business data is either locked down so tightly that employees can't do their jobs, or it's so open that anyone can see everything. You're either blocking productivity or risking data breachesâ€”there's no middle ground.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Over-Permissioning**: Employees have access to data they shouldn't see, creating compliance and security risks
- **Under-Permissioning**: Employees can't access what they need, creating bottlenecks and frustration
- **Manual Permission Management**: IT manually assigns permissions, creating delays and errors
- **No Audit Trail**: When something goes wrong, you can't tell who had access to what
- **Compliance Nightmares**: Regulatory requirements demand proof of access controls, but you can't provide it

- **Role Management**: Create, update, and manage roles with hierarchical structures
- **Fine-Grained Permissions**: Control access at resource, action, and field levels
- **Policy Engine**: Rule-based access control with complex business logic
- **Multi-Tenancy**: Company-level isolation with cross-tenant management

- **Complete Audit Trail**: Log of all permission checks and access decisions
