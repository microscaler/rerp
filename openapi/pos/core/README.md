## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Your point of sale is limited and disconnected. Cash registers are basic, inventory isn't synced, and you can't see sales in real-time. Restaurant table management is manual, orders get lost, and customer experience suffers. You're selling with limitations instead of selling with power.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Basic Cash Registers**: Limited functionality, can't integrate with other systems
- **No Real-Time Inventory**: Can't see inventory in real-time, sell out-of-stock items
- **Manual Table Management**: Restaurant table management is manual and error-prone
- **No Offline Capability**: System goes down, sales stop, revenue is lost
- **Limited Payment Options**: Limited payment methods reduce sales
- **No Sales Analytics**: Can't see sales performance or identify trends
