## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Managing employees is chaotic. Employee information is scattered across spreadsheets, files, and systems. Onboarding is manual, offboarding is forgotten, and you can't see your organizational structure. HR is drowning in paperwork instead of focusing on people.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Employee Data Scattered**: Employee information in multiple places, never matching

- **No Organizational Visibility**: Can't see organizational structure or reporting relationships
- **Document Chaos**: Employee documents (contracts, certificates) stored in filing cabinets
- **Offboarding Gaps**: Employee departures not properly tracked, access not revoked
- **No HR Analytics**: Can't track headcount, turnover, or organizational metrics
