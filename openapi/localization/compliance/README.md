## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Regulatory compliance is complex and risky. GDPR, CCPA, SOX, and other regulations require specific controls, but you can't prove compliance. Audit trails are missing, data retention is unmanaged, and you face penalties for non-compliance. You're operating at risk instead of operating with confidence.

**The Pain Points**:
- **Compliance Complexity**: Multiple regulations (GDPR, CCPA, SOX) with different requirements
- **No Audit Trails**: Can't prove who accessed what data or when
- **Data Retention Chaos**: Don't know what data to retain, when to delete, or how to archive
- **Access Logging Missing**: Can't log data access for compliance audits
- **Compliance Reporting**: Can't generate compliance reports for auditors
- **Regulatory Risk**: Non-compliance leads to penalties, fines, and reputation damage
