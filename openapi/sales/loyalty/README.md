## Why Your Business Needs This

**The Problem**: Customer acquisition is expensive, but you're not doing enough to keep customers coming back. One-time buyers never return, repeat customers don't get rewarded, and you can't track customer lifetime value. You're spending money to acquire customers who leave, instead of investing in customers who stay.

**The Pain Points**:
- **High Customer Churn**: Customers buy once and never return, acquisition costs are wasted
- **No Customer Rewards**: Repeat customers get no recognition, no incentives to return
- **Lifetime Value Unknown**: Can't track which customers are most valuable
- **Manual Loyalty Management**: Points tracked in spreadsheets, redemption is chaotic
- **No Loyalty Analytics**: Can't see which programs work or which customers engage
- **Competitive Disadvantage**: Competitors have loyalty programs, you don't
